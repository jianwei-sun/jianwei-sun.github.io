I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Bionics Lab at the University of California, Los Angeles, under the supervision of Prof. Jacob Rosen. My current research focuses on the control and estimation of an exoskeleton robot for physical human-robot interaction, in the context of automated physical rehabilitation. My research interests also include:

  • Admittance control for robotic systems
  • Pose estimation and sensor fusion
  • Control for nonlinear systems
